Course Overview

In this course you will learn about the history of cybersecurity, the types and motives of cyberattacks, in order to deepen your knowledge of the current threats facing organizations and individuals.Key terminology, concepts, and basic system tools will be examined as an introduction to the field of cybersecurity. You will learn about critical thinking and its importance for anyone who wants to pursue a career in cybersecurity. Finally, you will begin to learn about the organizations and resources that will allow you to further your research on cybersecurity issues in the modern era.

what you'll learn

Discuss the evolution of security based on historical events.

List the different types of malware.

Describe the key concepts of cybersecurity, including the CIA triad, access control, incident response, and common cybersecurity best practices.

Identify the main cybersecurity tools, including firewalls, antivirus software, cryptography, penetration testing, and digital forensics.
