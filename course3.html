Course Overview

This Specialization provides a rigorous treatment of spatial motion and rigid body dynamics, utilizing representations from modern screw theory and the exponential product formula. Undergraduate engineering students will rapidly learn to apply these tools to the analysis, planning, and control of robot motion. Students' understanding of robotics mathematics will be solidified through the writing of robotics software. Students will test their software on a free, cross-platform robot simulator, allowing each student to gain authentic experience in programming robots with industrial robot manipulators and mobile robots without the need to purchase expensive robotics hardware. It is strongly recommended to follow courses 1 to 6 of the Specialization in order, as the material builds upon itself.
